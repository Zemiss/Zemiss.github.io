# 数学分析II-第十一章 多元函数的微分学

## 11.1 偏导数

1

**题目：求下列函数的偏导数:**
1. $z = \left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^x + x^{\frac{y}{x}}$;
2. $u = \arcsin \frac{z}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}$;

**思路：**

$$
z=z_1+z_2
$$

$x$ 偏导

$$
z_1=\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^x
\Rightarrow
\ln z_1=x\ln\frac{x}{y}
\Rightarrow\frac{\partial z_1}{\partial x}=z_1\left(\ln\frac{x}{y}+1\right)
$$

$$
z_2=x^{\frac{y}{x}}
\Rightarrow
\ln z_2=\frac{y}{x}\ln x
\Rightarrow
\frac{\partial z_2}{\partial x}
=z_2\cdot \frac{y(1-\ln x)}{x^2}
$$

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}
=\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^x\left(\ln\frac{x}{y}+1\right)
+
x^{\frac{y}{x}}\frac{y(1-\ln x)}{x^2}
$$

$y$ 同理

$$
\ln z_1=x\ln\frac{x}{y}
\Rightarrow
\frac{\partial z_1}{\partial y}
=-\frac{x}{y}\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^x
$$

$$
\ln z_2=\frac{y}{x}\ln x
\Rightarrow
\frac{\partial z_2}{\partial y}
=x^{\frac{y}{x}}\frac{\ln x}{x}
$$

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}
=-\frac{x}{y}\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^x
+
x^{\frac{y}{x}}\frac{\ln x}{x}
$$

**题目：求下列函数指定的高阶偏导数:**

$$u = xyz e^{x+y+z},\frac{\partial^{p+q+r} u}{\partial x^p \partial y^q \partial z^r}$$

**思路：**
根据**高阶偏导的乘积法则**，混合偏导数满足：

$$
\frac{\partial^{p+q+r} u}{\partial x^p \partial y^q \partial z^r} = \frac{\partial^p A(x)}{\partial x^p} \cdot \frac{\partial^q B(y)}{\partial y^q} \cdot \frac{\partial^r C(z)}{\partial z^r}
$$

利用**莱布尼茨公式**（乘积的高阶导数公式）：

$$
(fg)^{(n)} = \sum_{k=0}^n \binom{n}{k} f^{(k)} g^{(n-k)}
$$

**条件：设 $f(r)$ 是二阶导数连续的一元函数, 记 $u = f(r), r = (x_1^2 + \dots + x_n^2)^{\frac{1}{2}}$,**

**求证:**

$$
\sum_{i=1}^n \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x_i^2} = f''(r) + \frac{n-1}{r}f'(r).
$$

**思路：**

$$
u=f(r)
\Rightarrow
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x_i}=f'(r)\frac{x_i}{r}
\Rightarrow
\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x_i^2}
=f''(r)\frac{x_i^2}{r^2}
+
f'(r)\frac{r^2-x_i^2}{r^3}
\Rightarrow
结论
$$




## 习题11(A)

5

**条件：设 $u = f(x,y)$，其中 $f(x,y)$ 的二阶偏导数连续。**

**引进极坐标 $x = r\cos\theta,\ y = r\sin\theta$，**

**求证：**

$$
\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} = \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial r^2} + \frac{1}{r^2}\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \theta^2} + \frac{1}{r}\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}.
$$


**思路：**

$$
(x,y)\to(r,\theta)
\Rightarrow
先求 r_x,r_y,\theta_x,\theta_y
\Rightarrow
代入链式法则
\Rightarrow
二次求导后合并
\Rightarrow
结论
$$

**条件：设 $f(u,v)$ 在 $\mathbb{R}^2$ 上二次连续可微且 $\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial u^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial v^2} = 1$。令 $g(x,y) = f\left(xy,\ \frac{x^2-y^2}{2}\right)$，**

**求证：**

$$
\frac{\partial^2 g}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 g}{\partial y^2} = x^2 + y^2.
$$


**思路：**

$$
g(x,y)=f\left(xy,\frac{x^2-y^2}{2}\right)
\Rightarrow
设,u=xy,\ v=\frac{x^2-y^2}{2}
$$

$$
\Rightarrow
链式法则求,g_{xx},g_{yy}
\Rightarrow
相加后交叉项抵消
\Rightarrow
代入,f_{uu}+f_{vv}=1
$$

**条件：设函数 $\varphi$ 的 $n$ 阶导数连续，$u = \varphi(z)$，其中 $z = \frac{x}{1-y}$，**

**求证：**

$$
\frac{\partial^n u}{\partial y^n} = \frac{\partial^{n-1}}{\partial x^{n-1}}\left(z^n \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right).
$$

**思路：**

$$
u=\psi\left(\frac{x}{1-y}\right)
\Rightarrow
先设 \xi=\frac{x}{1-y}
\Rightarrow
由链式法则得\ u_y=\xi u_x
$$

$$
\Rightarrow
用数学归纳法k=1\text{ 成立}
$$

$$
\Rightarrow
第k项表达式对 x 求导得到 k+1 情况成立
\Rightarrow
结论
$$
